1 ✧ choose°˖

the first step in creating your own display font is to choose...
let your mind run free and choose a material to work with

prompts:
  • choose your material
  • what has inspired you lately?
  • liquid, solid, or gas?
  • what material surrounds you these days?
  • are you reading a book that references certain material?
  • create a mind maps
  • list word associations to current fixations/ interests/ obsessions
  • what materials are connected to these things?
  • do not think about what could be turned into a font; just think about what materials and things are related to your interests [colours, shapes, sounds, textures...]
water example:

i chose to work with water as my material. i was inspired by the eight poem from the Tao Teh Ching, a Chinese classic text and foundational work of Taoism from roughly 4th century BC

poem:

“The highest form of goodness is like water.
Water knows how to benefit all things without striving with them.

It stays in places loathed by all men.
Therefore, it comes near the Tao.”

(Tao Teh Ching, 8)

disclaimer:

remove all preconceived notions of what your font will look like. do not have a final product in mind. if you have expectations, break up with them. i am warning you now that you will not make a font that you can currently imagine. following this method is meant to bring you to wildly unexpected outcomes.
